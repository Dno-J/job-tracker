👋 About Me

Hey, I'm Dino. I'm learning to become a better web developer by building projects that actually solve problems I'm facing.

This job/internship tracker started as a small idea to help me stay organized during my own job search. I didn’t want to rely on messy spreadsheets anymore, so I decided to build something of my own. Along the way, I picked up FastAPI, Tailwind, JWT auth, and worked on things like export features and charts just trying to build the full thing end to end.

I’m still figuring things out, but I’ve realized I learn best by doing not just watching tutorials. Each project teaches me something new, whether it’s backend logic, cleaner UI, or writing better code. Right now, I’m focused on growing as a developer, building more useful tools, and eventually contributing to real-world software teams.

If you're reading this and have feedback or want to connect, feel free to reach out!
